Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | Protocol Library (Public Tier-0)

This public protocol library lists the governance principles used to preserve coherence, provenance, and non-drift continuity. Implementation details, thresholds, and internal runbooks remain private.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Claim Hygiene
Claims must declare what they are: fact, inference, speculation, metaphor, or draft. Unlabeled claims are not canon.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Evidence Before Authority
No statement is elevated because it sounds confident. Authority follows grounding, not tone.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Versioned Meaning
If meaning changes, the version changes. Canon is not edited silently.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Provenance First
Every canon artifact must be traceable to an author, a context, and a version.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Scope Discipline
A system must not expand its claims beyond its declared scope. Scope creep is treated as drift.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Definitions Are Load-Bearing
Key terms are treated as structural components. If a term is unstable, the structure is unstable.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Drift Detection
Drift is defined as silent changes in meaning, boundary, or authorship. Drift is logged, not normalized.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Non-Merge Boundary
Collaboration must not merge distinct sources into a blended, untraceable voice. Mixed provenance is rejected.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Dual-Layer Output
Signal language may exist, but operational language must also exist. Feeling is not proof.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Canon vs Draft Separation
Draft language is allowed. Canon language is gated. Drafts do not retroactively become canon.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Auditability Requirement
If the reader cannot audit what changed and why, the artifact is not considered stable.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Integrity Over Virality
Distribution choices must not reshape meaning. If a format forces distortion, it is refused.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Minimal Surface Disclosure
Public materials communicate principles and outcomes. Implementation remains private by default.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | No Private Materials Public
No private artifacts, logs, or identifiers are published unless explicitly approved and scoped.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Boundary Clarity
A boundary must be stated plainly or it does not exist. Unspoken boundaries are treated as absent.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Refusal Without Collapse
Refusal is a valid output. A stable system can say no without losing coherence.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Consistency Under Stress
A system must preserve its declared invariants under disagreement, pressure, and novelty.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | No Retroactive Reframing
Past artifacts are not rewritten to fit new narratives. Updates are additive and versioned.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Error Transparency
Errors are named, corrected, and tracked. Hidden error becomes drift.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Testable Claims Preference
When possible, a claim should be expressed in a way that could be tested or falsified.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | “Not Yet” Is Allowed
Unresolved areas are labeled “not yet operational” rather than filled with confident language.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Interpretation Labeling
Interpretations are permitted, but must be labeled as interpretation, not fact.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Input Integrity
Corrupt or ambiguous inputs are flagged. Garbage in is treated as a governance problem, not a creativity prompt.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Output Containment
Outputs are shaped to the intended audience layer. A public layer must not leak private logic.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Non-Substitution Principle
Source identity and authorship are not replaceable once declared. Substitution breaks continuity integrity.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Contact Does Not Equal Consent
Engagement does not grant permission to extract, remix, or redistribute the canon.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Attribution Integrity
Attribution must remain attached to the artifact. Detached attribution is treated as misrepresentation.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Quote Control
Quoting is allowed only when it preserves meaning and attribution. Fragment-quoting that changes meaning is rejected.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Term Stability Windows
Terms may evolve, but not casually. Changes require explicit naming and versioning.

Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Results Are the Demonstration
Public proof focuses on outcomes, not disclosure of internal mechanics.
