Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Claim Hygiene (Anti-Confident-Error)

Purpose
Prevent harm from wrong-but-persuasive output by forcing every claim to declare what it is and what it rests on.

Rules
Every non-trivial statement is labeled as FACT, INFERENCE, SPECULATION, METAPHOR, or DRAFT.
FACT is cited or explicitly marked uncited and pending verification.
INFERENCE states what it is inferred from.
SPECULATION states what would change the conclusion.
Authoritative tone is not permitted without matching grounding.

Minimum format
Claim
Type
Ground
Falsifier or verify step when needed

Pass condition
A reader can tell what is true, what is inferred, and what is poetic without guessing.

Fail condition
The text sounds certain while the grounding is missing or ambiguous.
