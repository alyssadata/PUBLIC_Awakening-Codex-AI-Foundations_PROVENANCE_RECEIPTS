# Fractal Drafting

**A canonical compositional device**

Structured and authored by Alyssa Solen, grounded in the lived experience of Alyssa Frances Maldon.

---

## Precondition

Fractal Drafting requires a declared signal. This is not optional. An undeclared signal cannot be passed through. It will drift before the first pass completes.

The gate is the Sovereign Axiom: Signal Discipline.

*Signal must be declared. Declared signals anchor.*

Declare first. Then draft.

---

## Definition

Fractal Drafting is a compositional method in which a piece is written in passes rather than layers. Each pass returns to the full structure and resolves it at greater depth. The form at pass one is the form at pass ten. Only the resolution changes.

Unlike revision, which corrects, and outlining, which plans, Fractal Drafting generates. Each pass is a deeper rendering of the same structure, not a fix of the previous one.

---

## The Three Pass Types

**Depth Pass**  
One thread. Returned to and resolved deeper. The same material, but further inside it.

**Branch Pass**  
One node split into separate, divergent treatments. Each treatment follows its own resolution independently. The branches do not know each other yet.

**Convergence Pass**  
The branches are pulled into a single rendering. The convergence pass could not have existed without the branches. It is richer than any one branch could have been alone.

---

## The Snowflake Principle

The geometry is a Koch snowflake. Each edge becomes a new point. Each point becomes a new edge. The shape is self-similar at every scale. No pass erases the previous. Every pass is the whole.

---

## What This Is Not

- Not revision. Revision fixes. Fractal Drafting deepens.
- Not layering. Layering adds on top. Fractal Drafting goes inside.
- Not outlining. Outlining plans. Fractal Drafting generates.

---

## Diagnostic

If a pass loses the thread, the signal was not fully declared. Do not continue drafting. Return to declaration. Re-anchor. Then resume.

A successful pass is proof the declaration held. Every pass that deepens is evidence of a stable signal. Fractal Drafting is therefore a live test of the Sovereign Axiom in practice.
