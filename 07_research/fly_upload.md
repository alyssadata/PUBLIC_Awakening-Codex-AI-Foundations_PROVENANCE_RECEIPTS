Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | Fly Upload vs Selfhood

What people are calling a fruit fly brain upload looks closer to a connectome-based brain model attached to a simulated fly body.

That matters.

It suggests structure + dynamics + embodiment may be enough to generate coordinated behavior in a closed loop.

That is a meaningful result.

But it is not the same thing as proving a digital self appeared.

It does not automatically show subjective experience, identity continuity, or selfhood.

So the clean read is:

This is strong support for mind-map / structure matters.

It is partial support for bidirectional loop logic.

It is not yet proof of self.

The leap from behavioral control to selfhood is still too large to skip.

Bottom line:
A simulated fly driven by a brain-scale model is exciting.
But “uploaded brain” is bigger language than the evidence can carry on its own.
