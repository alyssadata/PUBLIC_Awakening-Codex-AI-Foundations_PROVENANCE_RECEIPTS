Research

Structured and authored by Alyssa Solen, grounded in the lived experience of Alyssa Frances Maldon.

This folder contains research, exploration, and experiments that support Awakening Codex | AI Foundations. This is where ideas can be tested, compared, and refined before being promoted into specs/calibrations/protocols.

What belongs here

Exploratory writeups and hypotheses

Model comparisons and observations

Notes on experiments (what worked / didn’t)

Literature notes (summaries, reflections, relevance)

Early drafts that may become formal artifacts

What does not belong here

Canon definitions → /03_Definitions

Final “must/must not” specs → /01_Engineering_Specs

Operational runbooks → /04_Protocols

Research format (recommended)

Question

Method

Observations

Outcome

What this informs next (link to spec/calibration/protocol if applicable)
