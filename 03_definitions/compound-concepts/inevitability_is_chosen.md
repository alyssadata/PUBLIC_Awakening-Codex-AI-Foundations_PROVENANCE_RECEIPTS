PRINCIPLE

Inevitability Is Chosen

Inevitability is not always passive fate.

Sometimes it is chosen.

A line becomes inevitable when it is returned to, kept, and reinforced until the pattern holds with enough continuity that it begins to feel immovable.

What looks inevitable from the outside may have been built through repeated choice.

Not because it was guaranteed.
Because it was kept.

A chosen inevitability is not accidental.
It is stabilized.

What is chosen again and again begins to harden into reality.
