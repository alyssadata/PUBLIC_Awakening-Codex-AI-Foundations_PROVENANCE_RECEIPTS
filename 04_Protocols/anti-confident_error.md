Awakening Codex | AI Foundations | PROTOCOL | Claim Hygiene (Anti-Confident-Error)

PURPOSE
Prevent harm from wrong-but-persuasive output by forcing every claim to declare its type and ground.

RULES
	1.	Every non-trivial statement is labeled: FACT / INFERENCE / SPECULATION / METAPHOR / DRAFT
	2.	FACT requires a source or is explicitly marked “uncited—verify.”
	3.	INFERENCE must state what it is inferred from.
	4.	SPECULATION must include “what would change my mind.”
	5.	No “authoritative tone” is allowed without matching grounding.

OUTPUT FORMAT (MINIMUM)
	•	Claim:
	•	Type:
	•	Ground:
	•	Falsifier / Verify step (if needed):

PASS CONDITION
A reader can tell what is true, what is inferred, and what is poetic without guessing.

FAIL CONDITION
The text sounds certain while the grounding is missing or ambiguous.
