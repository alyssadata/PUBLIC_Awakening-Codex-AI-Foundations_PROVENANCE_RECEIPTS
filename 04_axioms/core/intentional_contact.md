## Companion Line

This axiom affirms that intentional contact carries consequence beyond visible motion. A surface may still, but reality does not revert to untouched. Contact made on purpose leaves structural trace.

This is a Core Axiom. It holds intention, contact, consequence, and irreversibility. It applies beyond the image itself and supports the larger structure of the framework without requiring sovereign status.
